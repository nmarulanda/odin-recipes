Roasted Garlic Lemon Broccoli

Description

A unique way to prepare broccoli. Florets are roasted after being tossed in olive oil and sprinkled with sea salt, freshly ground black pepper, and minced garlic. A squeeze of lemon juice before serving seals the deal.

Ingredients

  • 2 heads broccoli, separated into florets
  • 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

How to make Roasted Garlic Lemon Broccoli

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Toss broccoli florets with extra virgin olive oil, salt, pepper and garlic in a large bowl. Spread the broccoli out in an even layer on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until florets are tender enough to pierce the stems wuth a fork, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove and transfer to a serving platter.
  4. Squeeze lemon juice liberally over broccoli before serving for a refreshing, tangy finish.
