Homemade Mac and Cheese

Description

This classic baked mac and cheese recipe with a buttered bread crumb topping is creamy and comforting. It's easy to make the cheese sauce from scratch on your stovetop, starting with a roux and adding milk, Cheddar, and Parmesan, resulting in a rich, decadent sauce that coats every nook and cranny of the noodles. Serve this comfort-food macaroni and cheese casserole with grilled meats, sloppy Joes, fried chicken or alongside a salad for a meatless dinner.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni/noodles
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings
  • Bread crumbs

How to make Mac and Cheese

  1. Boil the noodles in salted water until they are al dente. Drain and transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  2. For the cheese sauce melt butter and whisk in the floure. Then whisk the milk, bring to a simmer and stir the cheese. Season with salt and pepper and continue simmering until the sauce is thick. Put the sauce over the noodles and stir.
  3. For the toppings melt two tablespoons of butter in a skillet, add the bread crumbs and toast until the crumbs are brown. Spread the toppings over the macaroni and cheese, then sprinfle with paprika.
  4. Bake the mac and cheese in the preheated oven until the topping is golden brown
